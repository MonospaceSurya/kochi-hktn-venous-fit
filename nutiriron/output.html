DIET PLAN:

Breakfast:

  • Day 1: 1 cup idli with 1/2 cup sambhar and 1/4 cup coconut chutney + 1 small glass of milk (low-fat)
  • Day 2: 1 cup dosa with 1/4 cup sambar and 1/4 cup coconut chutney + 1/2 cup vegetable salad with lemon juice
  • Day 3: 2 slices of bread with 1 tbsp peanut butter (ensure it is peanut allergy friendly) + 1/2 cup fruits (apple, banana, papaya, etc.)

Lunch:

  • Day 1: 1 cup dal (moong, masoor, chana) + 1 cup rice + 1/2 cup vegetable curry + 1/2 cup salad with lemon juice
  • Day 2: 2 roti + 1 cup vegetable curry + 1/2 cup salad + 1 small cup rice (optional)
  • Day 3: 1 cup mixed vegetable salad + 1 cup mixed vegetable soup (optional)

Dinner:

  • Day 1: 2 chapatis + 1 cup vegetable curry + 1/2 cup salad + 1/2 cup paneer
  • Day 2: 1 cup rice + 1 cup dal + 1 cup vegetable curry + 1/4 cup fish/chicken curry
  • Day 3: 2 chapatis + 1 cup vegetable curry + 1/4 cup fish/chicken curry

Snacks:

  • 1 cup buttermilk/coconut water
  • 1 cup fruit salad
  • Handful of almonds/walnuts

Fluids:

  • Drink 2-2.5 liters of water per day.
  • Avoid sugary drinks.

ADDITIONAL TIPS:

  • Diabetes:
    • Monitor blood sugar levels regularly.
    • Include low glycemic index foods like fruits, vegetables, and whole grains.
    • Choose lean protein sources and limit saturated fats.
    • Consult a registered dietitian for personalized advice on managing diabetes.
  • Weight Loss:
    • Include plenty of fruits, vegetables, and nuts in your diet.
    • Avoid simple sugars and processed foods.
    • Choose whole grains and millets over refined flour.
    • Exercise regularly (jogging for 30 mins daily is a great start).

BASELINE DIET PLANS:

Class A (C0 and C1):

  • Diet Plan 1:
    • Calories: 1500 kcal
    • Carbohydrate: 55% of total calories
    • Fat: 20% of total calories
    • Protein: 0.8g/Kg body weight
    • Fiber: 30g
  • Diet Plan 2:
    • Calories: 1800 kcal
    • Carbohydrate: 60% of total calories
    • Fat: 25% of total calories
